# Warehouses

Warehouses represent physical locations where products are stored and shipped from. They are configured in the Merchello backoffice and define shipping origins, service regions (which countries/states they can ship to), and per-product stock levels.

Warehouses affect your storefront in two ways: they determine which shipping options are available for a customer's location, and they control product availability based on stock in reachable warehouses.
